Game play rules:

In single player mode, players have to navigate the maze in a given number of steps, and their scores are ranked first by the number of steps and then by time. Use up, down, left and right to control player movement

In two-player mode, each player takes turns walking each turn, and each turn has a maximum number of steps (7) as well as a time limit. The first player out of the maze wins and wins two out of three games.Use up, down, left and right to control player1 movement.Use W, S, A and D to control player2 movement
